What You'll Learn

The Election Ecosystem

Elections are complex multi-phase events involving citizens, candidates, and government bodies. Here's a bird's-eye view.

📋

Voter Registration

The foundation of democracy. Citizens must register before they can vote, verifying their eligibility and address.

Learn more →
🏛️

Candidate Filing

Aspiring candidates officially declare their intent by filing paperwork and sometimes paying a filing fee within set deadlines.

Learn more →
📣

Campaigns & Primaries

Candidates campaign, debate, and compete in primaries or caucuses to earn their party's nomination.

Learn more →
🗳️

Election Day

Citizens cast their votes in person or by mail. Polling locations open early and close at a set time in each jurisdiction.

Learn more →
📊

Counting & Canvassing

Votes are counted, verified, and audited. Canvassing boards certify the official results for each race.

Learn more →
🎖️

Certification & Swearing In

Winners are officially certified and take office. A peaceful transfer of power is a cornerstone of democracy.

Learn more →
